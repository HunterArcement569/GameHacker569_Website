Call of Duty: Zombies

Introduced to me at some point in 2012 by my soon-to-be step-brother, I instantly was both scared to death and deeply intrigued by this game and mode. Up to this point the only video games I had played were wii sports, mario, mario kart, and just dance (I regret that last one to my core). As such, this game has inspired many programs and given quite a lot to me personally in terms of entertainment. I have even been inspired to recreate it with Unity, though that project is progressing very slowly and has been scrapped for some time due to the lack of time to work on it consistently. Despite that setback, I still wish to recreate this game in a way that does the franchise justice.


Most of these are going to be streams as I can only record an hour of footage for a video at a time for edited videos. My favorite map is Origins and that will be somewhat apparent with how often I play it. Enjoy!
