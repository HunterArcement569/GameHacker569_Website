Overwatch

I started playing this game in late 2016, I have been addicted since. It is like a toxic relationship (though I have never been in a relationship let alone a toxic one), I hate the game at times because I know it can do better. I have seen my fair share of online friends groups form and disband over the years, the only thing that has changed is the rank we all are and the topics of conversation. I've gone from high silver/low gold to high diamond/low masters. I don't play as much anymore since my interest in games is in playing with others, so I do not play by myself.
